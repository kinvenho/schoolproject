Frequently Asked Questions

What deductions can I include in the tax calculator?

You can include deductions such as mortgage interest, charitable contributions, and medical expenses. However, the specific deductions available may vary based on your tax situation.

How often should I use the tax calculator?

It is advisable to use the tax calculator at least once a year before filing your taxes to get an estimate of your tax liability. You can also use it to estimate taxes for different scenarios, such as changes in income or deductions.

Can the tax calculator help me with tax planning?

Yes, the tax calculator can be a useful tool for tax planning by providing estimates for different scenarios and helping you make informed decisions about deductions and credits.

How does the tax calculator handle state taxes?

The tax calculator focuses on federal taxes. For state taxes, you may need to use a separate calculator or consult with a tax professional familiar with your state's tax laws.
